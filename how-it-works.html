How it Works

Have questions about your data? FeatureLab will help you find the answers.

Transform data

At its core, data science is about transforming raw data into useful representations. Even with the most advanced technologies, human involvement is still needed to brainstorm, implement, and evaluate ideas for representing data to a machine learning algorithm.

Through a process called Deep Feature Synthesis, FeatureLab combines new technology with old-fashioned human ingenuity to build these transformations automatically

Build optimized models

With a few clicks, FeatureLab automatically chooses optimal variables, builds appropriate models, and recommends the refinements best suited for your dataset. Armed with this knowledge, you can choose the best path forward.

Operationalize predictions

Your predictive model in hand, FeatureLab helps you put your data to use. It’s easy to highlight insights, test hypotheses with simulated inputs, and export batch predictions for use in other applications.

"FeatureLab starts with a high-fidelity, auto-generated model and gives you an innovative interface to improve the solution."
